Semantic Lyrics

An application of HTML, CSS and experssive typography on web.

  • Choose an expressive text. It could be a song lyric, poem, a manifesto, your own piece of writing, etc.
  • Also include a brief description of this text: the year, the author, why did you choose it.
  • Mark up your material in HTML.
  • Decorate your material with CSS.
  • Include at least 1 image, 1 hyperlink, 3 different text sizes.
  • Your design can stick to the meaning of the material, or go beyond with your personal interpretation.
  • Visual reference: What Screens Want
